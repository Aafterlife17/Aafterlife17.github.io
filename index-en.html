About us

In the manufacture of ARVAKO automatic packaging machines we follow a highly technical process. Our experienced human team knows how to correctly evaluate each order, so that the result of it responds to the trust that customers have placed in our company, and for this we have all the necessary technical means.

The rigorous control to which we submit each piece that we manufacture is one of our greatest strenght, when it comes to supporting the image and quality of our machines, which always bear the ARVAKO seal.
